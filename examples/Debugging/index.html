Debugging can get really complicated as the number of templates start to increase, and we tend to forget which template loaded which part of the page. For that we have included a handy little debugging script in the bin directory called JstDebugger.js. Just add it to your page after the JST.js and the JST engine will highlight the paths of the templates from which they were loaded.
